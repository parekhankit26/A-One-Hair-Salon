Email Setup

Configure EmailJS to send real booking confirmation emails to clients.

How to Enable Booking Emails

EmailJS lets you send emails directly from the browser — no server required. Free tier: 200 emails/month.

1

Create a free account

Go to emailjs.com and sign up for a free account.

2

Add an Email Service

In your EmailJS dashboard, go to "Email Services" and connect Gmail, Outlook, or any SMTP provider.

3

Create an Email Template

Go to "Email Templates" and create a template. Use these variables in your template:

To: {{to_email}} Subject: Your Booking at A One Hair Salon ({{booking_id}}) Dear {{to_name}}, Your appointment is confirmed! Booking ID: {{booking_id}} Service: {{service}} Stylist: {{stylist}} Date: {{date}} at {{time}} Estimated Price: {{price}} See you at {{salon_name}}! {{salon_addr}} | {{salon_phone}}
4

Copy your credentials below

Get your Public Key from Account settings, and paste your Service ID and Template ID here:

Your EmailJS Credentials

Paste your credentials here. They are saved locally and used in booking.html.

Email config saved! Update booking.html with your credentials.
