✂ EmailJS Setup Guide

2 email templates only — works perfectly with the free plan

Free plan = 2 templates max  |  template_admin_booking — Admin alert  +  template_cust_update — All customer emails
1Create a free EmailJS account

Go to emailjs.com → click Sign Up Free.
The free plan gives you 200 emails/month and 2 templates — exactly what we need.

💡 Sign up with info@aonehairsalon.com so everything stays in one place.
2Connect your email (create a service)

After logging in: Email Services → Add New Service → choose Gmail → sign in with info@aonehairsalon.com → name it Salon Email → click Create Service.

👉 Copy the Service ID — it looks like service_abc1234. You'll need it in Step 5.
