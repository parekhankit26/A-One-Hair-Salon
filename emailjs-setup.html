✂ EmailJS Setup Guide

3 email templates · real HTML buttons · direct customer emails · ~10 minutes to set up

1Create a free EmailJS account

Go to emailjs.com → Sign Up Free. The free plan gives you 200 emails/month.

💡 Use info@aonehairsalon.com to keep everything in one place.
2Connect your email (create a service)

After logging in → Email Services → Add New Service → choose Gmail → sign in with info@aonehairsalon.com → name it Salon Email → click Create Service.

👉 Copy the Service ID that appears (looks like service_abc1234) — you will need it later.
