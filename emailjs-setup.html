✂ EmailJS Setup Guide

Set up free email with real CONFIRM & REJECT buttons — takes about 10 minutes

1Create a free EmailJS account

Go to emailjs.com and click Sign Up Free.
Use your email and create a password. The free plan gives you 200 emails per month — plenty for the salon.

💡 Use info@aonehairsalon.com so everything stays in one place.
2Connect your email (create a service)

After logging in, click Email Services in the left menu, then Add New Service.

Choose Gmail (or Outlook if you prefer). Click Connect Account and sign in with info@aonehairsalon.com.

Give it the name Salon Email and click Create Service.

👉 Copy the Service ID — it looks like service_abc1234. You will need it later.

💡 If you see a yellow warning about Gmail, click "Click here to enable" — it opens Google, you accept, then come back.
3Create the ADMIN email template (with CONFIRM + REJECT buttons)

Click Email Templates → Create New Template.

Template Settings:

FieldWhat to enter
Template NameAdmin Booking Notification
Template IDType: template_admin_booking
SubjectNew Booking: {{service}} | {{from_name}} | {{date}} {{time}}
To Emailinfo@aonehairsalon.com
From NameA One Hair Salon Bookings
Reply To{{customer_email}}

Body (HTML) — copy everything below and paste into the Body field:

In EmailJS template editor, click the </> Code button (or switch to HTML mode) then paste this:

✂ A ONE HAIR SALON

NEW BOOKING REQUEST

📋 Booking Details

Booking ID{{booking_id}}
Customer{{from_name}}
Service{{service}}
Stylist{{stylist}}
Date{{date}}
Time{{time}}
Price{{price}}
Mobile{{mobile}}
Customer Email{{customer_email}}
Notes{{notes}}

Tap a button below — WhatsApp opens with the message ready, just tap SEND

✅ CONFIRM BOOKING    ❌ REJECT BOOKING

Hit Reply on this email to write directly to the customer.

A One Hair Salon | info@aonehairsalon.com

Click Save Template when done.

5Get your Public Key

In EmailJS, click your account name (top-right) → Account → General tab.

You will see your Public Key — it looks like aBcDeFgHiJk1234. Copy it.
